Privacy Policy & Guidelines

I. Introduction

Thanks for being interested in using Katzumi.
Before doing so, you should carefully read, understand and accept Katzumi's Terms of Service, then this Privacy Policy.

Your privacy is important to us. We will always inform you about how your data is used within our services in this Privacy Policy.

II. The information we receive

When an interaction is made on our Service we receive some information :

  • Public data provided by Discord
    When an interaction is made on a server where the bot is present, we receive public data which is provided by Discord. Information about your account (nickname, unique id, profile picture...), information about the server (name, unique id, change of nickname / roles...). Some of this data is processed automatically to enable the proper functioning of the Services but is not stored.
  • Message content
    When you send a message to a Discord server that has added Katzumi (or send a private message to Katzumi), you agree that the content of your message (which may contain confidential information) will be automatically processed by Katzumi in part of its proper functioning but are not stored.

All this informations are destroyed after 7 days.

III - Use of your data

The information provided is used to ensure the proper functioning of the services.
We do not access your information unless you give us permission (for example when requesting technical support).

The security of your information is our top priority.
Therefore, we will not sell or share your information with third parties.

IV - Disclosure of your information

We may disclose your personal information in response to a legal request or a request from public authorities, if required by law.
We will also cooperate with authorities to investigate suspected illegal activity.

V - Children's information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet.
We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.

Katzumi does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

VI - Your rights

In accordance with the laws in force, you have the right to request access, rectification and deletion of all or part of your data.

VII - Our rights

We have full permission to modify your biography and date of birth on the bot, if you put an explicit description with vulgar words, it will be reseted. If you put an anniversary date in the future, if it is more than 90 years ago, it will be reseted.

We can also blacklist you if we find that your use of our services or discord is malicious.

This privacy policy is valid for all services offered by Katzumi.
Additionally, Katzumi reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time.
We recommend that you check this page regularly to keep up to date with changes.
